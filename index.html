How it works

“Knowing yourself is the beginning of all wisdom.” – Aristotle
Discover your Type

Discover what type you identify with and begin to learn about how you see the world and operate in it.

Connect with Others

Find others who are similar and different than you and begin to understand how others see the world.

Learn to Communicate

When you better understand yourself, you better understand those around you. Learn how to effectively communicate with your family, your friends, and your coworkers.

Unlock your potential

Be confident in who you are wherever you go! The enneagram is this awesome tool that helps you put words to how you see the world. With our help and the use of this ancient super tool you will be able to clearly define who you really are, and learn to honor yourself. Learn how to apply your personal values and always strive to be your best self.



Strengthen your Relationships.

Compare your personality type with that of your partner or friends! Learn about your friends’ personality type and discover their strengths and weaknesses. Identify common frustrations and how to solve them. Get to understand the motivations and desires of your partner and friends better. See how you complement each other and how to get around your biggest roadblocks. Also gain personalised advice on how to improve your relationship.
